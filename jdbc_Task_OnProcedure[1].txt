


Program_01:
==========
Write a jdbc program according to below steps:
step-1 : Create the following DB Tables
StuData(stuId,stuRollNo,stuName,stuBranch)
StuAddress(stuId,stuHno,city,pincode)
StuContact(stuId,mid,phno)

step-2 : Construct Procedure to insert student details into DB Tables.
step-3 : Construct JDBC Application to execute Procedure.

ans:

  1  create or replace procedure studentdata( STUID  in NUMBER,
  2   STUROLLNO in NUMBER,
  3   STUNAME in  VARCHAR2,
  4   STUBRANCH    VARCHAR2,
  5   STUHNO  in varchar2,
  6   CITY    in varchar2,
  7   PINCODE in number,
  8   MID in varchar2 ,
  9  PHno in number)
 10  is begin
 11  insert into studata values(STUID,STUROLLNO,STUNAME,STUBRANCH);
 12  insert into stuaddress values(STUID,STUHNO,CITY,PINCODE);
 13  insert into stucontact values(STUID,MID,PHno);
 14* end;
 15  /

Procedure created.


Program_02:
==========
Step-1=>Create Table:Student_info
Columns:stuId,stuName,stuRollNo,stuAddress,stuMailId,stuPhoneNumber.
step-2 :Construct Procedure to display the complete details of student based on stuId.
step-3 :Construct JDBC Application to execute Procedure.


ans:

  1  create or replace procedure retrivestudentdata( ID  in NUMBER,
  2   ROLLNO out NUMBER,
  3   NAME out  VARCHAR2,
  4   BRANCH   out VARCHAR2,
  5   HNO  out varchar2,
  6   ci    out varchar2,
  7   pi out number,
  8   MI out varchar2 ,
  9  PH out number)
 10  is
 11  begin
 12  select STUROLLNO ,STUNAME , STUBRANCH into ROLLNO,NAME,BRANCH from studata where STUID=id;
 13  select STUHNO ,CITY , PINCODE into HNO,ci,pi from stuaddress  where STUID=id;
 14  select  MID   , PHNO into MI,PH from stucontact where STUID=id;
 15* end;
 16  /
